Maldives

It is neither inexpensive or simple to get to, but this remote Indian Ocean holiday location southwest of India epitomises a wonderful tropical escape. Thatched-roof overwater bungalows rise over the clear blue sea in this isolated resort of more than 1,000 islands, providing convenient water access and a romantic atmosphere. Fill your days on this once-in-a-lifetime holiday with beach excursions, spa treatments, and snorkelling or scuba diving excursions. If cabin fever strikes, head to Malé, the capital, where you’ll find mediaeval mosques and open-air markets.
